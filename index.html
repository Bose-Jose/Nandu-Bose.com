Step-by-Step Crab Cooking Guide

Cooking crab can be both fun and rewarding! Start by choosing fresh crab, boil it in salted water, and enhance the flavor with garlic butter or spicy masala. Serve hot and enjoy the juicy, tender meat with your favorite sides!
